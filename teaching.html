Teaching with AI: How can teachers leverage AI technology in their work?

Creative Writing Assistant

In this video demonstration, we explore the integration of ChatGPT into an elementary classroom setting, specifically tailored for enhancing storytelling and writing skills. The session begins with the teacher introducing ChatGPT to the students, explaining its capabilities and how it can assist them in creating and expanding their own stories.

Intelligent Grading Assistant

AI-powered tools can assist teachers in grading assignments, particularly for subjects like math and coding, where answers can be objectively evaluated. These tools can save teachers time and provide immediate feedback to students, allowing for faster iteration and improvement.

Virtual Teaching Assistants

AI-powered chatbots or virtual assistants can be used to answer common student questions, provide guidance on assignments, and offer additional support outside of class hours. This can help alleviate some of the workload for teachers while ensuring that students have access to the help they need.
